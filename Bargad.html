This is a brief of our Bargad trip

This was on of the most the most daring trips I ever accomplished. We bunked the hostel and spent a full night outside in another city. We originally studied in Burla near Sambalpur and it was not pertmitted to go out after 6 PM, but that day we all went out at 5 smuggling our bagpacks from the back of the hostel and hiding from almost everyone we went to the Hirakud station, took a train and in 30mins we were in our destination. We quickly went to the grounds where the MELA was organised. Its always a fun moment whatever we do when its with our friends. We took some snacks and went out to enjoy. That was the day when in 20 years I rode a giant wheel for the first time. Even for the first time I had a fire paan. Well its rightly said that with friends, fear always run away. We couldnt enjoy the infamous JATRA but had a lot of fun. We returned to the station to spend the night. It was really chilling. Lighting up a bonfire to sharing paranomal experiences. Dancing to the tunes of music to shivering in the cold. Every moment of it was enjoyable. In the morning we took a bus back to hostel and sneaked inside. Thats all to my first nightout in college with buddies. Sorry, not buddies, They are brothers.
